Problem 17.20

In the following problem, I use this notation:
^  for conjunction
v  for disjuntion
-  for negation
-> for implication
T  for true
F  for false 

Questions:
1. A ^ (-A v B v -C) ^ -C
2. (-A v -B v C) ^ -C
3. (-A v B) ^ (A v -B)

Solutions:
1. (T -> B) ^ ((A ^ -B ^ C) -> F) ^ (C -> F)
2. ((A ^ B ^ -C) -> F) ^ (C -> F)
3. (A -> B) ^ (B -> A)
